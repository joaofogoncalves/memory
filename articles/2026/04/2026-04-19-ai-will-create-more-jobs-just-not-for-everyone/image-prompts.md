## Hero Image

Photorealistic wide-angle architectural photograph of a monumental stone wall interrupted by a single tall, narrow doorway. The door itself is thick, heavy, slightly ajar — warm interior light spills out onto the pavement in a long rectangle. A small queue of people in contemporary dress stands along the wall in soft silhouette: two near the threshold, the rest trailing off into the shadowed foreground. Shot from a low, slightly off-centre angle on a 35mm lens, shallow depth of field on the figures, the stone texture and door in sharp focus. Cinematic color grade — cold overcast daylight on the exterior (slate blues, muted concrete tones), warm tungsten glow through the opening (amber, ochre). Early-morning atmospheric haze. No signage, no text. Composition leaves generous breathing room around the doorway so it survives 16:10 and square crops. Reference: Gregory Crewdson staged exteriors, Wim Wenders urban frames, *New Yorker* photo-essay openers. Avoid: painterly brushwork, illustration style, watercolor texture, 3D render kitsch, glowing sci-fi tech elements, bright tech-startup palettes.

Format: 1440x900px (16:10) · PNG
