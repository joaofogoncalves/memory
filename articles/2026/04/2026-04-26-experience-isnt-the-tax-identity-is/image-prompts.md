# Image Prompts

## Hero Image

Wide architectural photograph of a wood-paneled private library at dawn, taken from a low, slightly recessed vantage point near the back of the room. Tall arched windows on the right wall let first light spill across a long reading table in the middle distance. One figure is seated at the table, mid-frame, head turned slightly down toward an open book — hands on the page, a laptop open beside the book with a soft, neutral screen glow. Stacks of older books rise behind them along the walls, leather and cloth bindings catching the warm light. The figure's age is ambiguous; their posture is calm and absorbed, not theatrical. Shot in the documentary style of Edward Hopper paintings interpreted through the lens of contemporary architectural photographers like Edward Burtynsky or Hélène Binet — a real place, photographed with intent. 35mm full-frame look, shallow depth of field on the figure with the library receding into soft focus, subtle film grain. Palette: warm amber daylight against deep walnut and forest-green shadows, with one cooler highlight from the laptop screen anchoring the foreground. The figure is centered horizontally with generous breathing room on both sides so the image crops cleanly at 16:10 spotlight, full-width × 220px archive cards, and 300×175 list cards. No text, no readable code, no visible book titles. Communicates "the third group, working quietly before the world wakes up" — decades of priors meeting a new tool, without spectacle. Avoid: glossy stock photography, dramatic lens flare, neon or cyberpunk colors, painterly brushwork, motivational color grading, AI-generated "glowing tech" aesthetic, multiple people, faces in close-up, modern open-plan office tropes.
