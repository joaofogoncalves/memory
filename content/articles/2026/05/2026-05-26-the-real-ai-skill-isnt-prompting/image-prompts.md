# Image Prompts

## Hero Image

Documentary cinematic photograph of an empty corporate training room, captured from a low angle near the back of the room a few minutes after the session has ended. The projector is still warm and glowing, throwing a soft blue rectangle onto a faintly visible slide that reads "PROMPT TEMPLATE" in muted sans-serif type. Rows of empty chairs face the screen, with paper handouts left on a few of them — one chair has a handout slightly fallen to the floor. A laptop on the front podium is still open, screen dimmed. Mixed light: warm tungsten overhead bulbs on the back wall, cool projector blue washing the front of the room, daylight fading through a single window high up on the side. Realistic interior architecture — drop ceiling, neutral carpet, beige walls — not stylized. Mood: quiet, slightly melancholy, post-event stillness. Subject centered (the projector beam and screen are the visual anchor). Shot on a 35mm lens, shallow depth of field, slight haze in the projector light. Editorial register: think *Wired* feature photography or *The New Yorker* photo essays. Realistic and inhabited, not staged.

No text overlays beyond the faint slide text. No people. No exclamation marks of design — no neon, no glow effects, no stock-photo gloss. Avoid: digital-painting aesthetic, illustration, neural-network background, suit-and-glowing-brain compositions, AI-art defaults.
