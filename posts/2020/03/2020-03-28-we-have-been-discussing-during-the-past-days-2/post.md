# March 28, 2020

We have been discussing during the past days how we at Valispace can best help with the challenges of the COVID-19 pandemic that we are facing.
There are amazing people/engineers/doctors out there who are coming together to work on open source projects to help fight the virus and save lives (projectopenair.org, CoVentChallenge, etc).
To help them, we are opening up our engineering platform for teams to coordinate and work on open source projects in Valispace, and we have set up an internal team to support these efforts in any way we can. If you want to join a team or help out in any way, feel free to reach out to us at engineering-taskforce@valispace.com
