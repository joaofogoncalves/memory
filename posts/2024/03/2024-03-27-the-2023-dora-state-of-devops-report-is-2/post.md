# March 27, 2024

The 2023 DORA State of DevOps Report is in! 📊

Want to boost your team's performance and stay at the forefront of DevOps practices? The latest findings from the DevOps Research and Assessment (DORA) report are a goldmine of insights.

🔑 Key Takeaways:
1️⃣ High-performing organizations lead the pack with 200% faster lead times and 250% fewer change failures. Speed and reliability are their game.
2️⃣ User-centric focus is a hallmark. They incorporate user needs, maintain a culture of experimentation, and collaborate effectively.
3️⃣ Healthy, generative cultures rule. Empowering employees, reducing bureaucracy, and enabling risk-taking are key.
4️⃣ Automation is a productivity driver. Investing in tools and processes that automate manual tasks is a game-changer.

📐 Metrics
𝗘𝗹𝗶𝘁𝗲 performers deploys on demand - 18%
𝗛𝗶𝗴𝗵 performers deploy between once per day and once per week - 31%
𝗠𝗲𝗱𝗶𝘂𝗺 performers deploy between once per week and once per month - 33%
𝗟𝗼𝘄 performers, deploy between one week and one month - 17%

🚀 Capabilities for High Performance:
- Continuous delivery: The ability to get changes of all types—including new features, configuration changes, bug fixes, and experiments—into production, or into the hands of users, safely and quickly, and sustainably.
- Reliability practices: Activities and practices teams use to improve the operational performance of services.
- Trunk-based development: The practice of making small, frequent changes that are regularly merged into the main code branch of the version control system.
- Flexible infrastructure: Scalable infrastructure that is elastic, accessible, and measured.
- User-centrism: Understanding and incorporating users' needs and goals to make products and services better.
- Generative culture: A culture that is proactive, innovative, and values learning.

🚀 Actionable Advice:
- Understand your users deeply.
- Invest in continuous delivery and reliability practices.
- Embrace trunk-based development and flexible infrastructure.
- Cultivate a user-centric and generative culture.

🔍 Additional Insights:
High-performing orgs automate with diverse tools.
They foster collaboration and knowledge sharing.
Training and development are a priority.

Remember, DevOps is a journey, not a destination. Find what works for your team and keep improving. Let's keep the DevOps revolution going!
