# March 27, 2024

"The Magic Loop" from Ethan Evans and Lenny Rachitsky! 

Have you ever wondered how to systematically grow in your career? 📈 The Magic Loop, as outlined in this insightful podcast article, can work wonders for your professional development. Here's a quick breakdown of the five basic steps:

1️⃣ Start by excelling in your current role. 🚀
2️⃣ Then, proactively ask your manager how you can be of more help. 🤝
3️⃣ Once assigned a task, give it your all. 🏆
4️⃣ As you build trust, discuss how you can contribute to your career goals while benefiting your team. 📊
5️⃣ Finally, repeat the loop to continually grow and collaborate effectively. 🔄

The magic in this loop lies in building a collaborative, mutually beneficial relationship with your manager. 🌟

So, if you're looking to level up your career, why not give the Magic Loop a try? It's worked wonders for many, including myself. 🌠
