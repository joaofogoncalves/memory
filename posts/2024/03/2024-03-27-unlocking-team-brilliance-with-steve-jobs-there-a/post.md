# March 27, 2024

Unlocking Team Brilliance with Steve Jobs🚀

There are many Steve Jobs riveting videos snippets online, but mostly are about design, product or marketing because he was without any doubt a master of those topics. But a less mentioned aspect is his Leadership, in this video he unveils a true gem:

"The greatest people are self-managing. Once they grasp the 'what,' they'll uncover the 'how'... Leadership is a common vision, articulated for collective understanding, forging consensus."
Echoing the Mac's ethos, Jobs extols the virtue of "insanely great" individuals. 

Key Takeaways:

𝗦𝗲𝗹𝗳-𝗠𝗮𝗻𝗮𝗴𝗲𝗺𝗲𝗻𝘁 𝗧𝗿𝗶𝘂𝗺𝗽𝗵𝘀: Great minds don't need constant management. They thrive with a shared vision.

𝗜𝗻𝘀𝗮𝗻𝗶𝘁𝘆 𝗶𝗻 𝗘𝘅𝗰𝗲𝗹𝗹𝗲𝗻𝗰𝗲: Build a team of individuals insanely great at what they do. It's the nucleus of success.

𝗥𝗲𝗰𝗿𝘂𝗶𝘁 𝗳𝗼𝗿 𝗜𝗺𝗽𝗮𝗰𝘁: Jobs emphasizes the paramount role of recruiting—getting the first ten right resonates far beyond, akin to hiring a hundred.

𝗥𝗲𝗰𝗿𝘂𝗶𝘁𝗶𝗻𝗴 𝗪𝗶𝘀𝗱𝗼𝗺: As @Leader, understand the multiplier effect. Each recruit isn't just one but a gateway to ten more.

Leadership isn't just about guiding; it's about sparking brilliance. Let your vision illuminate paths for your team. 🌟

Visionary leadership breeds excellence. Are you recruiting for the next tenfold impact?
