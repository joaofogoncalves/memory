# March 27, 2024

🚀 Why have I been posting so much lately? 🚀

No, it's not because I suddenly became a social media enthusiast, and it's not a result of boredom during my vacation. The truth is, I've always thought there is immense value in sharing information, knowledge, and lessons learned with anyone who wants to listen/read. 
Now I'm just (finally) taking action on it. 🏃‍♂️ 

🧠 Writing is my secret weapon for self-improvement. It forces me to organize my thoughts and articulate them clearly in English (not my native language). This exercise, in turn, enhances my ability to communicate effectively in person, which is crucial in my role as a Head of Technology.

📚 Moreover, it creates a valuable backlog of insights and experiences. Whether it's new team members, potential hires, investors, or customers trying to understand our approach, this repository of thoughts becomes a resource for everyone involved with Valispace (Acquired by Altium).

💡 Knowledge sharing is at the core of our industry. As leaders in software engineering, we understand the power of collaboration and collective learning. By sharing what we know, we inspire others to do the same, fostering a culture of continuous improvement.

So, the next time you see me pop up in your feed, know that it's not about vanity or self-promotion—it's about strengthening our community and empowering each other through the exchange of ideas. Let's keep the conversation going! 🤝
