# June 30, 2025

Here's something I learned during the Valispace (Acquired by Altium) Altium® acquisition that no leadership book prepared me for.

The hardest part wasn't merging codebases or aligning roadmaps. It was helping two engineering cultures become one while keeping what made each team special.

People don't just need to know what's changing - they need to understand why it matters and where they fit in the bigger picture. As a leader, you become a cultural translator, bridging gaps between different ways of working.

The teams that thrived were the ones where leaders took time to explain not just the new processes, but the thinking behind them. They helped people see how their unique strengths would contribute to something bigger.

Culture integration is the real challenge in any merger.
