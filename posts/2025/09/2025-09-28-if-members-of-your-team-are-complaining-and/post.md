# September 28, 2025

If members of your team are "complaining" and telling you what is not working for them, please pay attention, and do not ignore or dismiss it.
It means they care.

But when eventually they stop, don't think things are ok, mostly likely they just stoped caring and that's when they are most likely to leave.

If you car about team retention, it's the quiet ones you need to care about the most.
