# February 25, 2026

Claude Code's creator, Boris Cherny recently shared how his team actually uses it.

Their #1 unlock? 
Running 3-5 parallel sessions, each with its own Claude instance.

Other highlights:
→ Building and maintaining a CLAUDE md file that teaches Claude YOUR preferences
→ Custom slash commands for repetitive tasks
→ Using subagents for complex problems
→ Voice dictation (you speak 3x faster than you type)

The best developers aren't avoiding AI — they're becoming power users of it.

Credit to Carl Velloti on X, who broke it down into this cheatsheet.
