# February 25, 2026

I can confirm that AI has completely transformed how I approach major refactoring.

We've successfully cut costs by 80% while maintaining 100% of the anxiety. Some teams even report HIGHER anxiety levels, which really speaks to the efficiency gains.

People ask me: "Are you worried AI will replace developers?"

I tell them: absolutely not. Someone still needs to mass-click "approve" on 847 AI-generated pull requests and mass-sign the incident reviews.

The future is here. It's faster, cheaper, and exactly as terrifying.
