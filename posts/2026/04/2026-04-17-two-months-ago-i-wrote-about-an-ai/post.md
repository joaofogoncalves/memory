# April 17, 2026

Two months ago I wrote about an AI pipeline BRIDGE IN that could deliver a feature end-to-end. Spec in, PR out, one hour.

This Tuesday my laptop was closed. Sentry alert at 10:17. A skill created the issue, linked it to a week-old Slack thread, queued it. By 11:02 CI was green. Thumbs-up from my phone. Merge.

The pipeline didn't go away. It became one organ of something bigger.

Pipelines deliver. Nervous systems sense, react, remember, adapt. Sensors watching Sentry and Slack. Reflexes that claim work and kick off builds. Memory that reviews the week and proposes changes to the agents themselves.

The gap between "AI writes code" and "AI delivers features" was a systems problem. The gap between "AI delivers features" and "AI runs a product loop" is the same shape, one level up.
